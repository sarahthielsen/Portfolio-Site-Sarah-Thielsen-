VOCABEO

MY Role

As a new student at CareerFoundry, I had the opportunity to individually create a vocabulary app that empowered users to learn new vocabulary in a short time frame and on the go. During this independent project, my responsibilities ranged from defining the parameters of the problem, conducting initial market and user research, developing personas and corresponding user flows, as well as designing, prototyping and testing low to midfidelity wireframes.

Role

Lead UX Designer, UX Research,

Tools

Marvel, OptimalSort

Tiemline

February - March 2022

THE OBJECTIVE

Empower people to learn new vocabulary.

Problem

Learning a new skill, and learning how to learn, is a critical life skill no matter you age, location or circumstances. However, life is busy, and picking up a new skill - let alone learning a new language- can be a daunting task for many adults attempting to learn again.
