A bit more about me

I practice the art of the pivot...

I don't believe in sticking to what you're good at if your heart isn't in it! I went to university with the goal of becoming a nurse from 2017-2021, but in that time I grew so much and realized it wasn't for me.

PIVOT!

I then decided to embrace my creative side and became a licensed nail tech during the pandemic... I soon realized that wasn't for me either.

PIVOT!

These experiences are what ultimately led me to become a UX Designer! I found my love for helping people, my love for art and creativity, but most importantly, I fell in love with the process of it all. I carry these lessons with me in my design thinking, which allows me to push the envelope when creating design solutions.
